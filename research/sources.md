## Transcript Sources

During this project, I explored the Supadata transcript API to understand how YouTube transcript collection could be automated.

**Supadata Documentation**

https://docs.supadata.ai/get-transcript

Key documentation notes:

- Endpoint: `GET https://api.supadata.ai/v1/transcript`
- Authentication: `x-api-key`
- Preferred mode: `mode=native`
- Alternative mode: `mode=auto` when native captions are unavailable

Native caption availability was verified before collecting transcripts. When captions were available, YouTube's native transcript feature was used to ensure accurate transcript collection while also exploring how the workflow could later be automated using the Supadata API.

## Research Notes

The purpose of this document is to provide transparency into how the research was conducted. Rather than relying on a single source, this project combines insights from multiple SEO professionals across different platforms to identify recurring themes, compare viewpoints, and document practical observations.

Throughout the project, I focused on understanding how experienced practitioners use AI to support SEO workflows rather than simply generating content. The research reinforced the importance of combining AI with human judgment, search intent, and original insights to create content that delivers long-term value.
